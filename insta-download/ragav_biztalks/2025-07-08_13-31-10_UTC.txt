🎯 Client: Health & Wellness Brand – Tamil Nadu
🎯 Audience: Age 40+
 
We identified a key market gap through surveys and implemented a focused strategy that drove a 20x increase in sales. Here's the breakdown:
✅ Conducted a survey targeting 40+ age group
✅ Found knee pain as a top concern among respondents
✅ Proposed & introduced organic solutions to address the issue
✅ Collected and leveraged real customer feedback & UGC
✅ Created educational top-funnel content to build awareness
✅ Focused on trust, relevance & genuine problem-solving
🎯 Result: 20X Sales Growth 🚀
💡 Lesson: Identify the gap → Offer real solutions → Educate the market → Convert with trust.
